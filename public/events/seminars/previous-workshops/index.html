Previous Workshops and Programs

2011-01-24

Here are some of the previous programs we have conducted for other organizations:

Rapier Wit
2-day weekend intensive workshop (8 hours), 6 week program of 2-hour classes, and stand-alone workshops.

"Excellent class, well organized, well-taught, and a lot of fun." Erik Buchanan martial arts fight choreographer Toronto, ON
 
Academy of Martial Arts A series of 8-week programs of 2-hour classes.

Combat Science
A 1-hour workshop on kenjutsu for their "5x5 Martial Arts Extravaganza" (June 2006)

"A fabulous day of high powered training honoring five great styles by five awesome instructors – all for one terrific cause! All proceeds to support our Canadian competitors at the WEKAF World Championships in Florida this July."


Mixed Martial Arts Expo
A series of 30 minute introductory workshops.

“The seminar was great. It was very in-depth. I think I’m hooked. Thank you.” Chris Burlington, ON

Dragon Fencing Academy A series of 1-hour introductory workshops, 4 week programs of 1 hour classes, and 8 week programs of 2 hour classes.
"I am a long time student of kendo and iaido. Recently, I participated in a workshop on the basics of Katori Shinto Ryu kenjutsu with Douglas Tong. I was especially impressed with the pleasant and capable manner with which Tong Sensei led the large and diverse group. It was an energizing and fun experience. I am sure that anyone involved in traditional Japanese martial arts or swordsmanship in general will find much of interest in this style, which is possibly the oldest koryu (classical school) still in existence."
James Broadwell 5th dan, Muso Jikiden Eishin Ryu Oshawa Eishin-Ryu Iaido Kai
