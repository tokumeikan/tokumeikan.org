Haru Matsuri 2019 Demonstration

2020-02-11

Haru Matsuri 2019 Demonstration Report

copyright © 2019 Douglas Tong, all rights reserved

__________________________________________________

On March 2, 2019, the Japanese Canadian Cultural Centre in Toronto held its annual Spring Festival which is called Haru Matsuri. Once again, we were blessed to be allowed to participate in this wonderful celebration of Japanese culture and tradition.

This year, we needed to film our progress in our studies. Consequently, we had only three groups participating: Yagyu Shingan Ryu Taijutsu, Ono-ha Itto Ryu, and Yagyu Shinkage Ryu Heiho.

The audience got to see some great demonstrations from these famous koryu styles.

Below are a few samples of the videos that we filmed that day.
