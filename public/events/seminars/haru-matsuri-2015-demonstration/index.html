On March 7, 2015, we participated in the annual Spring Festival, called Haru Matsuri, at the Japanese Canadian Cultural Centre in Toronto.

This year, we needed to get some formal photographs of our work.

So, part of this demonstration was a photo shoot.

A big thank you to Dayman Perry for helping to photograph us.
