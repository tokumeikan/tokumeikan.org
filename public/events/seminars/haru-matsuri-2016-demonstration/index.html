Haru Matsuri 2016 Demonstration

2020-02-11

Haru Matsuri 2016 Demonstration Report

copyright © 2016 Douglas Tong, all rights reserved

__________________________________________________

On March 5, 2016, the Japanese Canadian Cultural Centre in Toronto held its annual Spring Festival which is called Haru Matsuri. We were fortunate to be allowed to participate in this high-class event.

This year had a new twist. Instead of just running the demonstration with our own group as we had been doing for the past decade, we experimented this year with inviting another group to come and demonstrate with us, the Toronto Niten Kai Kenjutsu Dojo from downtown Toronto.

It turned out to be a big success.

In all, we had the following groups participating: Yagyu Shingan Ryu Taijutsu, Hyoho Niten Ichi Ryu, Ono-ha Itto Ryu, and Yagyu Shinkage Ryu Heiho.

Each group put on an excellent demonstration of their art. ?

We had photographers who came to capture our demonstration on film.

And we were filmed as well by professional film crews.

In this ad from Tourism Ontario, you can catch the briefest glimpse at the 9 second mark of Phil McClorey and Tong Sensei demonstrating at this 2016 Haru Matsuri festival.

Haru Matsuri 2016 was an extraordinary event. Our heartfelt appreciation to everyone who participated and helped out!
